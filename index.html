About Section

About BreatheBetter India

BreatheBetter India is a citizen-driven initiative to combat air pollution through awareness, action, and community engagement. We believe that every individual has the power to make a difference in creating cleaner air for future generations.

Our mission is to educate, empower, and unite Indians in the fight against air pollution. Through technology, community action, and policy advocacy, we're working towards a future where every breath is clean and safe.
